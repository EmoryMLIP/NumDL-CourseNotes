	Let $E$ be the cross entropy, $\bfW_k$ the current weights, and $\bfD$ the search direction.  The line search problem is:
	\begin{equation*}
		\min_{\alpha > 0} \phi(\alpha) \quad \text{ where } \quad \phi(\alpha) = E(\bfW_k + \alpha \bfD).
	\end{equation*}
